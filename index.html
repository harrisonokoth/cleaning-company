About Us

Why Choose Your Clean Pal Co?

Your Clean Pal Co is a professional cleaning service business that provides exceptional cleaning services to the residents of Kilifi. Established in February 2025 by Fresiah Kanyiri, we aim to change the cleaning service industry by providing professional, exceptional and quality cleaning services.

Our purpose is to offer services that create clean and organized spaces for better living. We envision being the number one go-to cleaning pal that every home can trust.

We are trustworthy and faithful, providing quality service that is customer-centered, professional, reliable, responsible, and integrous.
